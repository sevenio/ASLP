Moved Files:
------------

Eclipse projects. Here's how the projects were restructured:

* AndroidManifest.xml => downloader_library/src/main/AndroidManifest.xml
* res/ => downloader_library/src/main/res/
* src/ => downloader_library/src/main/java/
